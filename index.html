What is the purpose of prototyping?

  • To test the ideas and (in)validate the hypothesis
  • To avoid committing the heavy investment in wrong assumptions

The higher the fidelity, the more efficient you can test an idea. But we can't afford to always build the real thing. Remeber the "Big Dig"?
