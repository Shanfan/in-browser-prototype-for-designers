What is the purpose of prototyping?

To test the ideas and (in)validate your hypothesis

To avoid investing in wrong assumptions

The higher the fidelity, the more accurate you can test an idea.

But we can't afford to always build to the highest fidelity.

How to prototype, and

what tool to use,

is a decision between

cost and test accuracy

What is the right prototyping tool for you?

Quick to get the idea across

Easy to iterate upon
